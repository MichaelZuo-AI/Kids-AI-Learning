和 AI 一起创作 Create Together with AI

人机协作：AI 是助手，不是替代品 Human-AI collaboration: AI is your assistant, not your replacement

🤔

如果 AI 可以帮你做所有事情，
你还需要动脑筋吗？ If AI could do everything for you,
would you still need to think?

我们来讨论一下！ Let's discuss!

你和 AI 的关系 Your Relationship with AI

🎬
你 = 导演 DirectorYou = Director
创意来自你
你决定做什么、怎么做 Creativity comes from you
You decide what to do and how
🎭
AI = 演员 ActorAI = Actor
执行靠 AI
AI 按你的想法去完成 Execution by AI
AI carries out your ideas

没有导演，演员不知道演什么！ Without a director, the actor doesn't know what to perform!

三种合作模式 Three Collaboration Modes

🌱
你想，AI写You Think, AI Writes
你有想法，让 AI 帮你表达 You have the idea, AI helps you express it
🔄
AI起稿，你修改AI Drafts, You Refine
AI 给初稿，你来完善 AI gives first draft, you make it better
💡
AI建议，你决定AI Suggests, You Decide
AI 提供选项，你做决定 AI offers options, you make the call
"最好的创作，是你的想象力加上我的能力。少了你，我只是在复制别人的东西。你才是创作的灵魂！" "The best creations combine your imagination with my abilities. Without you, I'm just copying others. You are the soul of creation!"

如果 AI 帮你做所有作业… If AI did ALL your homework…

📉
学不到东西You Don't Learn
做作业是为了让你学会呀 Homework is how you learn new things
🌱
不会成长You Don't Grow
遇到困难才能变强 Facing challenges makes you stronger
💪
大脑要锻炼Brain Needs Exercise
大脑就像肌肉，越用越强 Your brain is like a muscle — use it or lose it!

AI 应该帮你学得更多，而不是学得更少 AI should help you learn MORE, not learn LESS

这些事，只有你能做！ Things ONLY YOU Can Do!

❤️
有真实感受Real Feelings
开心、难过、感动…
这些是真的 Joy, sadness, wonder…
These are real
🌟

你的故事，

one of a kind

什么是对、什么是错，
你有自己的判断 Right and wrong —
you have your own compass
👨‍👩‍👧
真正爱你的人People Who Love You
家人的爱，

AI can never give
