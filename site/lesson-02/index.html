AI 怎么学习的 How Does AI Learn?

了解 AI 学习的秘密：训练数据 Discover the secret of AI learning: Training Data

🤔

你是怎么学会认字的？ How did you learn to read?

你看了很多很多的字，慢慢就认识了！
你看到"猫"这个字很多次，就知道它长什么样了。 You saw lots and lots of words, and gradually learned them!
You saw the word "cat" many times, and learned what it looks like.

AI 学习的方式和你一样！ AI learns the same way you do!

给 AI 看 1000 张猫的照片
它就学会了猫长什么样！ Show AI 1000 photos of cats
and it learns what a cat looks like!

这些例子有个名字：训练数据 (Training Data)
= 我们给 AI 看的例子 These examples have a name: Training Data
= the examples we give AI to learn from

教小狗 vs 教 AI Teaching a Puppy vs Teaching AI

🐕
教小狗Teaching a Puppy
给零食 → 说"坐下" → 反复练习 → 学会了！ Show treat → Say "sit" → Repeat → Learned!
🤖
教 AITeaching AI
给数据 → 找规律 → 反复练习 → 学会了！ Show data → Find pattern → Repeat → Learned!

它们都需要大量的重复练习才能学会！ Both need lots of repetition to learn!

🟣
"我学会超能力也不是一天就会的！训练师给我越多练习，我就越强。AI也是一样——数据越多，学得越好。" "I didn't learn Psychic in one day! The more my trainer practiced with me, the stronger I got. AI is the same — more data means better learning."

AI 学习三步走 Three Steps of AI Learning

📦
① 收集数据① Collect Data
给AI看很多照片/文字 Show AI lots of photos/text
🔍
② 找规律② Find Patterns
AI发现猫都有尖耳朵 AI notices cats have pointy ears
✅
③ 学会了！③ Learned!
看到新照片也能认出猫 Can recognize cats in new photos

收集 → 找规律 → 学会！就这么简单 Collect → Find patterns → Learned! That simple

AI 学认动物 AI Learns Animals

点击卡片，看看 AI 是怎么认出它们的！ Tap a card to see how AI recognizes them!

🐱
猫咪Cat
AI看到：
4条腿 + 尖耳朵
+ 胡须 + 毛茸茸
→ 猫！ AI sees:
4 legs + pointy ears
+ whiskers + furry
→ Cat!
🐕
小狗Dog
AI看到：
4条腿 + 垂耳朵
+ 尾巴摇 + 毛茸茸
→ 狗！ AI sees:
4 legs + floppy ears
+ wagging tail + furry
→ Dog!
🐟
小鱼Fish
AI看到：
没有腿 + 有鳍
+ 住在水里 + 有鳞片
→ 鱼！ AI sees:
No legs + has fins
+ lives in water + scales
→ Fish!
🦅
老鹰Eagle
AI看到：
2条腿 + 有翅膀
+ 有羽毛 + 会飞
→ 鸟！ AI sees:
2 legs + has wings
+ feathers + can fly
→ Bird!

  • 2 训练数据就是我们给 AI 看的例子Training Data is the examples we show AI
