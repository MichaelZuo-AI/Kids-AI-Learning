AI 帮我画画 AI Draws for Me

学会写 Prompt，让 AI 画出你脑海里的画面 Learn to write prompts and make AI draw what you imagine

🤔

你脑海里有一幅画，
怎么让 AI 画出来？ You have a picture in your mind.
How do you tell AI to draw it?

AI 看不到你的想法——你需要用文字描述给它！ AI can't see your thoughts — you need to describe them in words!

Prompt（提示词）= 你给 AI 的描述 Prompt = Your Description for AI

🎬
你是导演You're the Director
你决定画什么、怎么画
你说了算！ You decide what to draw & how
You're the boss!
🎨
AI 是画师AI is the Artist
AI 按照你的描述画画
导演说得越清楚，画越准确 AI draws based on your description
Clearer instructions = better art

导演说得越清楚，画师画得越准确！ The clearer the director, the more accurate the artist!

好 Prompt vs 坏 Prompt Good Prompt vs Bad Prompt

❌
坏 PromptBad Prompt
"画一只 Pokemon"

AI 不知道什么颜色、什么形状、什么背景… "Draw a Pokémon"

AI doesn't know the color, shape, or background…
✅
好 PromptGood Prompt
"画一只蓝色的、会喷冰的、很凶猛的龙形 Pokemon，背景是雪山"

AI 完全知道该怎么画！ "Draw a blue, ice-breathing, fierce dragon Pokémon with a snowy mountain background"

AI knows exactly what to draw!
🟣
"你描述得越详细，AI 画得越像你想要的！就像你跟我说招式一样——说'用超能力'太模糊，说'用精神强念对着喷火龙'我才知道怎么做！" "The more detail you give, the closer AI gets to what you want! It's like telling me a move — saying 'use psychic power' is too vague, but saying 'use Psystrike on Charizard' tells me exactly what to do!"
