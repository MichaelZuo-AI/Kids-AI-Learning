AI 擅长回答什么？ What Can AI Answer?

🔬
科学问题Science Questions
为什么天是蓝的Why is the sky blue
📜
历史故事History Stories
恐龙是怎么灭绝的How did dinosaurs go extinct
➗
数学题Math Problems
帮你解释和计算Help explain and calculate
🌐
翻译语言Language Translation
中文、英文、韩文…Chinese, English, Korean...
"但记住，就连我也不是完美的。AI也会犯错，重要的事情要和大人确认哦！" "But remember, even I'm not perfect. AI makes mistakes too. For important things, always check with a grown-up!"

重要习惯：三步验证法 Important Habit: Three-Step Verification

1️⃣
AI说了什么？What did AI say?
先理解AI的回答 First understand AI's answer
2️⃣
这听起来合理吗？Does it sound reasonable?
用你已知的知识判断 Use what you already know
3️⃣
有没有其他地方也这么说？Can you find it elsewhere?
查书或问大人 Check a book or ask a grown-up
