AI 帮我讲故事 AI Tells Stories

学会引导 AI，创作属于你的故事 Learn to guide AI and create your very own stories

🤔

如果你能让 AI 给你讲一个故事，
你希望故事里有什么？ If AI could tell you a story,
what would you want in it?

一条龙？一个魔法森林？还是你和超梦的冒险？ A dragon? A magical forest? Or an adventure with Mewtwo?

AI 写故事需要三要素 AI Needs 3 Elements to Write a Story

👤
主角 HeroHero
谁是故事的主人公？ Who is the main character?
🗺️
地点 PlacePlace
故事发生在哪里？ Where does the story happen?
⚡
事件 EventEvent
发生了什么事？ What happens?

告诉 AI 这三样东西，它就能给你写故事！ Give AI these three things and it can write your story!

好提示 vs 坏提示 Good Prompt vs Bad Prompt

❌
太模糊Too Vague
"给我讲个故事"

AI 不知道你想要什么样的故事… "Tell me a story"

AI has no idea what kind of story you want…
✅
超清楚Super Clear
"讲一个 Damian 和 Mewtwo 在冰雪森林里寻找消失的精灵球的故事"

AI 知道该怎么写！ "Tell a story about Damian and Mewtwo searching for a lost Poké Ball in an icy forest"

AI knows exactly what to write!
"我可以是你故事里的伙伴！告诉我你想要什么冒险，我帮你创造它。记住，最好的故事来自你的想象力！" "I can be your partner in any story! Tell me what adventure you want, and I'll help you create it. Remember, the best stories come from your imagination!"

让故事更精彩的魔法词 Magic Words to Make Stories Better

🌪️
加入一个惊喜转折Add a Twist
让故事出现意想不到的变化Create an unexpected change
🏆
让结局出人意料Surprising Ending
结尾让人"哇！"An ending that makes you say "Wow!"
📝
帮我续写下一段Continue the Story
让 AI 接着你的故事写下去Let AI keep writing from where you left off
😂
用搞笑的方式写Write It Funny
让故事充满笑点！Make the story hilarious!

🎯 互动时间🎯 Activity Time

用 3 张魔法词卡设计你的故事！ Design Your Story with 3 Magic Cards!

第1步：从上一页选 3 个魔法词卡
第2步：把它们组合成一个故事提示
第3步：念给爸爸听
第4步：让 AI 写出来！ Step 1: Pick 3 magic word cards from the previous slide
Step 2: Combine them into a story prompt
Step 3: Read it to Dad
Step 4: Let AI write it!

比如：讲一个有「转折」+「宝物」+「友谊」的冒险故事！ Example: Tell an adventure story with "Twist" + "Treasure" + "Friendship"!

今天学到了 ✨ What We Learned ✨

  • 1 AI 写故事需要三要素：主角 + 地点 + 事件AI needs 3 elements for a story: Hero + Place + Event
  • 2 描述越具体，AI 写得越精彩The more specific your description, the better AI writes
  • 3 用魔法词让故事更有趣：转折、惊喜、搞笑…Use magic words to make stories exciting: twists, surprises, humor…
  • 4 最好的故事来自你的想象力，AI 只是帮你写出来The best stories come from your imagination, AI just helps write them down
