问题越清楚，答案越好 Clear Questions = Better Answers

学会给AI下好指令（Prompt） Learn to give AI great instructions (Prompts)

😵‍💫

我说一个模糊的指令，
看你能不能理解？ I'll give a vague command —
can you understand it?

👉
"帮我拿那个""Get me that thing"
哪个？？？Which one???
🎨
"画一个好看的""Draw something nice"
什么好看的？？？Nice how???
🚶
"去那边""Go over there"
哪边？？？Where???

你知道我在说什么吗？是不是很困惑？ Do you know what I mean? Confusing, right?

超梦也需要清楚的指令！ Mewtwo Needs Clear Commands Too!

❌
"帮我打""Help me fight"
超梦：??? 打谁？ Mewtwo: ??? Fight who?
✅
"用超级念力打那只喷火龙！""Use Psychic on that Charizard!"
超梦：没问题！⚡ Mewtwo: Got it! ⚡

AI 也是一样 —— 清楚的指令 = 很棒的结果 AI is the same — clear instructions = great results

好 Prompt 的四个要素 Four Elements of a Good Prompt

👤
Who 谁Who
主角是谁 Who is the main character
📋
What 做什么What
具体任务 The specific task
🎨
How 怎么样How
风格/方式 Style / approach
🎯
Why 为什么Why
目的/场合 Purpose / occasion
"一个好的指令，能让我发挥最强的能力。模糊的指令，让我不知所措。记住：说得越清楚，AI做得越好！" "A good prompt lets me use my full power. Vague prompts leave me confused. Remember: the clearer you speak, the better AI performs!"

Prompt 进化之路 Prompt Evolution

❌ v1 "帮我写个故事" "Write me a story" 太模糊Too vague
⬇️
🔶 v2 "帮我写个关于Pokemon的故事" "Write me a story about Pokemon" 好一点Better
⬇️
✅ v3 "帮我写一个Damian和Mewtwo在海底寻找传说神兽的5句话短故事，要有惊喜结局" "Write a 5-sentence story about Damian and Mewtwo searching for a legendary Pokemon underwater, with a surprise ending"
完美！🎯Perfect! 🎯

三个魔法词让 Prompt 升级 Three Magic Words to Upgrade Any Prompt

📝
"详细地""In detail"
让AI回答得更完整 Makes AI answer more completely
👶
"用5岁孩子能懂的语言""In kid-friendly language"
让AI说得简单易懂 Makes AI explain simply
💡
"举个例子""Give me an example"
让AI用例子帮你理解 Makes AI use examples to help

加上这些词，答案马上变好！ Add these words and answers instantly improve!
