AI 也会犯错 AI Makes Mistakes Too

了解 AI 的局限，学会独立思考 Understand AI limitations and think critically

🤔

你有没有见过大人犯错？
老师犯错？爸爸犯错？ Have you ever seen adults make mistakes?
Teachers? Even Dad?

犯错是正常的！每个人都会犯错——AI 也一样！ Making mistakes is normal! Everyone makes them — and so does AI!

AI 会犯的三种错误 Three Types of AI Mistakes

🌀
幻觉 HallucinationHallucination
AI 有时候会"乱说"，
编造不存在的事实 AI sometimes "makes things up"
— invents fake facts
📅
知识过时Outdated Knowledge
AI 学习的数据
有截止日期 AI's training data
has a cutoff date
⚖️
偏见 BiasBias
AI 学的数据有
人类的偏见 AI can reflect
human biases in its data

验证三步法 The 3-Step Check

🔎
① 问一问 Ask① Ask
这是真的吗？
AI 说得对吗？ Is this true?
Is AI correct?
🤔
② 想一想 Think② Think
根据你知道的，
有道理吗？ Based on what you know,
does it make sense?
📖
③ 查一查 Check③ Check
问爸爸、看书、
或查可靠来源 Ask Dad, read a book,
or check a trusted source

重要的事情，一定要验证！ For important things, always verify!
